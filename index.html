Privacy Policy

Naadha Abhyas is a specialized Carnatic music practice tool designed for swara accuracy, pitch detection, and musical growth.

Effective Date: March 2026

Information We Collect

Naadha Abhyas requests access to the device microphone. This is strictly required to analyze your voice in real-time for pitch detection and swara identification.

We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or location data.

How We Use Information

Audio captured is processed locally on your device. The data is used instantaneously to provide musical feedback.

The application never records, stores, or transmits your audio data to any external servers or cloud services.

Data Storage & Security

Because we do not collect data, there is no personal data stored on our end. All processing happens within the temporary memory of your device and is cleared immediately.

Third-Party Services

Your privacy is sacred. We do not share data with any third-party advertisers, analytics companies, or service providers.

Children's Privacy

Naadha Abhyas is safe for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
