Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Naadha Abhyas mobile application (“App”, “we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Scope

This policy applies to the Naadha Abhyas Android application built with Flutter, including its integration with third-party services such as Google Firebase, Google Sign-In, and Cloudinary. The App is designed as a Carnatic music practice companion with features such as tanpura, metronome, pitch guide, and swara detection.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account and Profile Information

The current version of Naadha Abhyas does not require you to create an account or sign in. We do not collect your name, email address, or profile photo through in-app sign-in flows at this time.

2.2 Usage and Practice Data

  • Audio recordings: Your voice or instrument recordings are primarily stored locally on your device for analysis (pitch detection, breath control, etc.).
  • Generated media: Audio and video generated from notation or tala practice are stored on your device and only shared if you choose to export them.
  • Practice configurations: Selected tonic (Sa), tempo, tala, and notation settings are stored to maintain your session.

2.3 Device and Technical Information

Through SDKs (Firebase, device_info_plus), we may collect:

  • Device model, OS version, and characteristics.
  • App version and language settings.
  • Network connectivity status.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens for push notifications.

3. Permissions We Request

3.1 Microphone

Used for real-time audio analysis (pitch, swara, volume) to provide musical feedback. We do not use microphone access for advertising or hidden recordings.

3.2 Storage / Media Access

Used to read and write practice outputs (generated audio/video) and allow you to pick profile images via the system gallery. We only access media you explicitly choose.

3.3 Notifications

Used to deliver push notifications regarding updates or reminders. You can revoke this in device settings.

3.4 Foreground Service & Wake Lock

Used to keep the tanpura, metronome, or recording sessions running reliably even when the screen is off or the app is in the background.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following services to provide app functionality:

  • Google Firebase: Used for Authentication, Firestore data management, and Cloud Messaging. Data is processed according to Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sign-In: If used, we receive basic profile info (name, email) to manage your account.
  • Cloudinary: Used for secure storage and delivery of uploaded media assets.

5. Advertising

Naadha Abhyas does not display ads. The app does not use the device Advertising ID (AD ID) and does not integrate with any ad-tracking SDKs.

6. Children's Privacy

Naadha Abhyas is a music practice app and may be used by students, including minors, often under the guidance of a teacher. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent. If you believe a child has provided data without consent, please contact us.

7. Data Storage & Security

All audio and video created for practice is stored locally on your device. Account data or uploaded media are stored using trusted cloud providers (Firebase/Cloudinary). While we use secure channels, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Choices and Rights

You may control permissions (microphone, notifications) via your device settings. You can delete practice files by clearing app data. To request deletion of any account-related data stored in our back-end systems, please contact us at the email below.
