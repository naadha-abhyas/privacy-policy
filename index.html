Privacy Policy

Naadha Abhyas is a Carnatic music practice application designed to help users improve their swara accuracy, pitch control, and understanding of musical notes through interactive practice tools.

Effective Date: March 2026

Information We Collect

Naadha Abhyas may request access to the device microphone. This permission is required to analyze the user's voice in real time for pitch detection and swara identification during practice sessions.

The application does not collect, store, or share personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or location.

How We Use the Information

Audio captured through the microphone is processed locally on your device only for the purpose of detecting pitch and providing musical feedback.

The application does not store, record, or transmit audio data to any external servers. Your practice remains private.

Data Storage & Security

Since Naadha Abhyas does not collect personal data, there is no risk of data exposure. All audio analysis happens in real-time and is discarded immediately after processing.

Third-Party Services

We do not share any data with third-party services, advertisers, or analytics providers.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for general audiences and music students. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
