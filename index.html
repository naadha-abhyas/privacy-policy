Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Naadha Abhyas is a Carnatic music practice application designed to help users improve their swara accuracy, pitch control, and understanding of musical notes through interactive practice tools.

Information We Collect

Naadha Abhyas may request access to the device microphone. This permission is required to analyze the user's voice in real time for pitch detection and swara identification during practice sessions.

The application does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or location.

How We Use the Information

Audio captured through the microphone is processed locally on the user's device only for the purpose of detecting pitch and providing musical feedback.

The application does not store, record, or transmit audio data to any external servers.

Data Storage

Naadha Abhyas does not store personal or audio data. All audio analysis happens locally on the user's device and is discarded immediately after processing.

Third-Party Services

Naadha Abhyas does not share user data with third-party services or advertisers.

Children's Privacy

This application does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is intended for general audiences interested in learning Carnatic music.

Security

Since the application does not collect or store personal data, there is minimal risk of data exposure. All processing occurs directly on the user's device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time to reflect improvements or changes in the application. Updates will be posted on this page.
